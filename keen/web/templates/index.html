How Keen Works

1

Safely share telephone records, emails, mailing lists, point-of-sale system data and social media profiles with Keen. Keen will keep your records secure in an encrypted contact database, automatically identify potential customers, and enhance your customer profiles with relevant data.

2

Keen’s proprietary algorithm will automatically identify key customer segments, suggest relevant offers and promotions for each segment, and create targeted email, SMS and social media promotional campaigns to drive customers to your store.

3

Keen will constantly monitor the performance of your customer engagement efforts and your promotional campaigns, analyze and recommend improvements along the way, and enable you to review progress online using a real-time dashboard.
