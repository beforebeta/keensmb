Keen is a Customer Relationship Management (CRM) Platform that helps your local business acquire more customers and learn more about the ones you already have:
Enhanced Customer Profiles
Automatically analyze online and offline communication and append customer profiles with third-party data, increasing the size and quality of your customer database.
Tailored Offers and Promotions
Our proprietary technology creates personalized offers and promotions tailored to your customers’ buying preferences.
Email Campaigns
Engage with your customers over email and drive them back with targeted promotional offers.
Mobile/SMS Campaigns
Motivate your customer base to visit your store by sending alerts and offers over SMS.
Social Engagement
Improve your social presence by engaging with facebook fans, yelp reviewers and twitter followers.
Performance Analysis
Identify your best customers and evaluate the performance of your promotional campaigns.
Connect with your customers directly by sending them automated email promotions

Keen helps you easily send promotional emails directly to the customers in your database. Keen creates the emails automatically and lets you approve the emails before they are sent out. The Keen dashboard helps you monitor the performance of each email promotion you send.

Emails are sent to the customers in your customer database list. You can also import customer email addresses from popular email programs like MailChimp and Constant Contact directly into your customer database list in Keen.
Let potential customers find you online and easily increase the size of your customer database

Keen helps you easily create simple websites that give your business a place on the internet. These sites help potential customers find you online and helps you increase the size of your customer database at the same time. With Keen you get better at acquiring new customers and grow your business!

Easily Monitor The Performance of Your Promotions

Keen’s dashboard lets you to monitor the performance of your promotion campaigns, learn more about your customers and make better decisions.
